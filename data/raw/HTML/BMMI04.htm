  Error code : BMMI04 Alarm Message: Hopper Low level 

Alarm Description
Product inside the hopper is lower that set level.

Reason & Remedy

1. The product level gets low in the hopper
    a. Insert the product into the hopper.
2.The sensor is not working correctly.
    a. Check the sensor and set it correctly. ? Refer to Chapter "Maintenance" > Section "Setting the Capacitive Sensor."
