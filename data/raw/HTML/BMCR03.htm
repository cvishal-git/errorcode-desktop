  Error code : BMCR03 Alarm Message: Heater MCB (5,6,7,8,9,22) / MPCB Trip  

Alarm Description
Heater or Vacuum Pump MCB/MPCB tripped in control panle. MCB/MPCB protaction signal not received in PLC. Check input X22 on Input Card-1.

Reason & Remedy

1. The MCB or MPCB switches off.
    a. Check loose wire connection in the control panel MCB or MPCB.
    b. Switch on tripped MCB or MPCB.
 
(Heater MCB)
2.MCB or MPCB tripped after turning it on.
    a. Check the heater of the sealing unit, heating unit, perforation unit and Powerpack unit wiring.(refer the wiring diagram)
